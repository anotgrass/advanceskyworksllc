About Advance Skyworks

Founder & Owner.

Hello, my name is Matt Sole and I am the owner of Advance Skyworks LLC. With being in the roofing industry for over 20 years now, I know the value drones can bring to a business. This is why I decided to start Advance Skyworks; to help businesses leverage a growing technology. I have been in the drone industry for a year but have been around general aviation all my life. I am FAA Part107 Certified and my number one goal is to stay devoted in taking care of my clients.

Services

Here at Advance Skyworks we are all FAA Part107 sUAS/Drone Licensed. We are also committed to take great pride, respect, and give our utmost loyalty in exceeding your needs. Here is what we can offer you:

Safety

We can provide safer ways to view roofs, whether residential or commercial, so you won't have to gain access to dangerous conditions.

Inspections

Provide inspections that can be done autonomously due to critical safety issues, as well as show you if there are significant signs of damage present.

Progress Verification

Help you check on employees/contractors to verify progress on roofs and other construction sites.

Imagery & Photography

Provide imagery for real estate agencies or other industries to give a different perspective in their portfolio.

Freelance Requests

Any other requests or needs that must be met, we are willing to negotiate and go over the neccessary plans or requirements if able to do so.
